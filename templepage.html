Pashupatinath Temple
During my college project, We have developed a fully functional website dedicated to showcasing the cultural and spiritual significance of the Pashupatinath Temple. The website was thoughtfully divided into three major sections: Home, Gallery, and Contact. The Home section provided visitors with an engaging overview of the temple’s history, importance, and key features, serving as the primary landing page. The Gallery section offered a visually rich experience by displaying a well-organized collection of images, capturing various aspects of the temple, its architecture,rituals, and surroundings, which helped to immerse users in the temple’s atmosphere. Lastly, the Contact section was designed to enable easy communication between visitors and our team, incorporating user-friendly forms and clear contact information to facilitate inquiries and feedback. Throughout the development process, careful attention was given to ensuring a responsive and accessible design, with intuitive navigation and aesthetic appeal, aimed at providing users with a seamless and informative browsing experience.
